Our Story

A working horse barn
where the unexpected happens

Crazy Girl Farm is a working equestrian barn on Vashon Island where horses still live and events happen alongside them, not instead of them. Pop-up dinners between the stalls. Live music under the chandelier. Wine tastings by candlelight while the horses watch from their gates.

Vashon does things a little differently. The ferry ride is part of the experience. The ingredients come from down the road. And the barn doors open to forest, pasture, and the kind of quiet that only island life can offer.

The Property
01

The Barn

A working equestrian barn with cedar stalls, hand-forged iron hardware, vaulted ceilings, and a chandelier that sets the mood for everything from intimate dinners to acoustic sets.

02

The Events

Farm-to-table pop-up dinners, wine and cider tastings, seasonal supper clubs, live music nights, weekend brunches, and maker markets in the stalls. No two events are the same.

03

The Island

Collaborations with Vashon farmers, winemakers, and artists. Ingredients sourced within miles. No rush, no pretension. The kind of place that only exists on an island.
